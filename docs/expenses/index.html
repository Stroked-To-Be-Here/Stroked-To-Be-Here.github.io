Monthly Expenses: How Your Support Helps Ella Thrive

Ella’s journey to recovery has been nothing short of miraculous, but it’s also come with significant financial challenges. Every month, these expenses ensure that she can continue her progress, regain independence, and live with dignity.

Here's where your contributions make a difference:

  • Home Care Assistance: $2,000/month
    Ella requires dedicated support to assist with daily activities and ensure her safety.
  • Groceries: $600/month
    Nutritious meals are a key part of her recovery and well-being.
  • Rent: $600/month
    A safe and stable home is essential for her ongoing rehabilitation.
  • Utilities: $250/month
    These basics keep Ella comfortable as she focuses on her healing.
  • Physical Therapy: $500/month
    Professional therapy sessions help her regain strength and mobility.
  • Acupuncture: $220/month
    Two monthly sessions bring relief and aid in her recovery journey.
  • IPSI Hand Therapy: $600/month
    This specialized therapy is critical for improving her left arm’s mobility—unfortunately, it’s not covered by insurance.
  • Miscellaneous Medical Costs: $500/month
    These cover co-pays, prescriptions, and other therapies vital to her recovery.

Your Impact

Every donation, no matter the size, brings Ella closer to regaining her independence and quality of life. Imagine being part of a community that helps Ella take her first steps toward a new milestone, like writing with her left hand or doing everyday tasks we often take for granted.
